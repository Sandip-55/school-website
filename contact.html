Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our school

Admission Process

Q: When does the admission process begin?
A: Our admission process typically begins in March for the academic year starting in April.

Q: What documents are required for admission?
A: You'll need previous year's mark sheet, transfer certificate, citizenship/birth certificate, and passport-sized photos.

Academic Programs

Q: What streams are available in +2?
A: We offer Science and Management streams at the +2 level.

Q: Do you offer scholarship programs?
A: Yes, we offer scholarships to meritorious students and those from economically disadvantaged backgrounds.

School Facilities

Q: What sports facilities are available?
A: We have basketball, volleyball, and badminton courts, table tennis, and a football field.

Q: Is transportation available?
A: Yes, we have school buses covering major routes in Kathmandu valley.

About Us

Manasalu College is one of the premier education institutions, which was established in 2002. It is situated at Nayabazar, strategically important location in the Kathmandu valley.
