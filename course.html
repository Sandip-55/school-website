Our Courses

Academic Programs

We offer a variety of courses designed to prepare students for higher education and professional careers.

Secondary Education (Grade 9-10)

Our secondary education program follows the national curriculum with a focus on building strong foundational knowledge in core subjects including English, Nepali, Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies.

2 Years SEE

Science Stream (+2)

Our Science program offers specialized courses in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics, preparing students for careers in medicine, engineering, research, and technology fields.

2 Years NEB

Management Stream (+2)

The Management program provides comprehensive business education with subjects like Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies, and Marketing, preparing students for careers in business and finance.

2 Years NEB

Curriculum Details

Our curriculum is designed to provide a balanced education that combines academic excellence with practical skills.

Science Curriculum

Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, English, Nepali, Computer Science, and Environmental Science.

Management Curriculum

Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies, Marketing, English, Nepali, Computer Science, and Mathematics.

Extracurricular Activities

Sports, debate, music, art, coding club, science fair, educational tours, and community service programs.
