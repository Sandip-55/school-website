About Us

We are Kathmandu's Leading School

Manasalu Public Secondary School is a well-established educational institute with a crystal vision of quality education to cope with the challenges of the 21st century. It was established in 2002 AD by a group of well-experienced educationists under the management of Manasalu Educational Institute Pvt. Ltd.

It endeavors to combine comprehensive, scientific, integrated moral education, friendly modulated by human values. The school pursues its goal of academic excellence, the overall development of the students, the talents in extra-curricular activities and co-curricular activities, and formation of character. It has achieved impressive growth and recognition since its establishment as one of the reputed members among private schools.

Manasalu Public Secondary School has been running +2 in Science and Management with the affiliation from the National Examinations Board (NEB).
